About me

A bug hunter ensuring flawless function beneath the surface.

Hi everyone, I'm Kalani Rodrigo! I'm an IT undergraduate at Rajarata University of Sri Lanka, and I’m passionate about quality assurance and software engineering. Sure, I’m still learning the ropes, but that just means I bring fresh energy and a sharp eye for detail to every project. I love breaking things (on purpose!) just to make them better and more reliable for real users. I know the path to becoming a great QA engineer takes focus and patience, but I’m ready for it. With curiosity, the support of my Rajarata community, and a never-give-up mindset, I’m excited to build software that truly works — and works well! ✨
